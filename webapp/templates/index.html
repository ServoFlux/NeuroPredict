Predict white matter disease & its likely cause

This is a multimodal model: it combines the brain MRI with a short clinical & genomic questionnaire. It predicts whether white matter disease is present and, if so, its likely cause — vascular, autoimmune, genetic, metabolic, or infectious. Upload a scan and answer the questions; the result page shows how much each source contributed and suggests next steps.
