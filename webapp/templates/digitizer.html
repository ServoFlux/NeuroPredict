Archive MRI digitizer

Many older studies only exist as physical film — a sheet printed with a grid of slice images. The Archive Digitizer companion (an ESP32-CAM over a light box) photographs that sheet; the server rebuilds the slices into the 3D volume this website already uses, then runs the normal prediction. The hardware simply helps the website get its input — it doesn't change the model.
